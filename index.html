After losing everything in a fire that destroyed my childhood home—including my prized collection of 47 rubber ducks and a half-eaten grilled cheese sandwich I was saving for later—I learned that the only things that truly matter are the connections we build, the impact we leave behind, and always finishing your sandwiches when you have the chance. Now, I pour my heart into every line of code, hoping to create something meaningful that might help someone, somewhere, feel a little less alone (and also to honor the memory of Sir Quacksalot, the bravest rubber duck who ever lived).
